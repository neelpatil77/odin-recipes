Lasagna

Description

This is the recipe for a killer vegetarian lasagna.

This lasagna leaves you satisfied, and contains veggies such as squash and bell peppers.

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  1. Cut the veggies and shred the cheese
  2. Layer veggies, cheese, sauce and pasta and bake covered for a while
  3. Enjoy!
